About

Skeletons in the Cupboard is a collection of short, personal, fictions by me, Aliya N. Anindita a.k.a. PseudoMon. Some of them are science fiction, some are fantasy, some are surrealist nonsense, all of them are bite-sized and can be read in one sitting.

This is the web version of the book. For more details and to download the e-book version, go to the Itch page.
